Pitch On the Beach 

Tech communities emerge on a daily basis. However, not everyone is able to keep up with these changes. How to make tech developers, product, marketers and entrepreneurs listen to the needs of customer in a more sustainable and calm manner?

“Pitch on the beach” aims to empower individuals to create a right pitch that can help them to improve their business idea and a personal and professional life. Join our first edition in the Netherlands or tune in via podcast.
